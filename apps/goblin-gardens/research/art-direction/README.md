## üé® The 3 Art Style Options

### Option 1: Stylized Low-Poly 3D
**"Chunky Goblincore"**
- Inspired by: Deep Rock Galactic, Goblin Stone
- Best for: Premium feel, 3D pipeline available
- Timeline: 5-8 weeks initial
- **When to choose:** You want depth, have 3D expertise, flexible timeline

### Option 2: Cartoon-Casual Vector ‚≠ê RECOMMENDED
**"Trash Treasure Tycoon"**
- Inspired by: Plants vs. Zombies, Clash of Clans
- Best for: Fast iteration, broad appeal, mobile optimization
- Timeline: 3-4 weeks initial
- **When to choose:** Speed, performance, and accessibility are priorities

### Option 3: Hand-Drawn Dark Whimsy
**"Burton's Goblin Garden"**
- Inspired by: Don't Starve, Goblincore aesthetic
- Best for: Unique identity, indie positioning
- Timeline: 5-6 weeks initial
- **When to choose:** Atmosphere, story, and standing out are goals

## üí° Primary Recommendation

**‚Üí Option 2: Cartoon-Casual Vector**

**Why?**
1. ‚úÖ Fastest time to market (3-4 weeks)
2. ‚úÖ Best mobile performance (lightweight vectors)
3. ‚úÖ Matches stated inspirations (PvZ, Clash)
4. ‚úÖ Broadest audience appeal
5. ‚úÖ Easy to iterate and add content
6. ‚úÖ Strong branding potential for Reddit community

**Consider Hybrid:** Use Option 2 as base + Option 3 aesthetics for rare items = visual hierarchy + uniqueness

## üìä Key Findings Summary

### Goblin Representation
- **Mythology:** Mischievous, greedy, treasure-obsessed European folklore creatures
- **Modern media:** Usually small, green, big ears, chaotic energy
- **Goblincore aesthetic:** "Dark cottagecore" - earth tones, foraging, trash to treasure, mushrooms, chaos

### Design Principles Alignment
From your `docs/design-principles.md`:
- ‚úÖ Goblincore aesthetic matches perfectly
- ‚úÖ Inspirations (PvZ, Clash, Deep Rock) inform all 3 options
- ‚úÖ "Trash to treasure" mechanic is central to goblin identity
- ‚úÖ "Number go up" + "power fantasy" feelings guide progression

### Platform Considerations (Reddit Mobile Client)
- Mobile-first design critical
- Lightweight assets preferred
- Fast loading essential
- Accessible to wide age range (12-45)
- Community/social features important
