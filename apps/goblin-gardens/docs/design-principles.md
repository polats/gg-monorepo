# dvg

IP:

Dwarves vs. Goblins is set a meta-fictional world where Dwarves and Goblins are constantly at war, but in a comedic way. Lots of meta fourth wall breaking references, self-referential on what Dwarves and Goblins are in popular media.

Keywords / Feelings / Vibes:
Dwarves = Studious, Engineering, Boisterous, Beards, Taverns, Mead, Mining, Forge

Goblins = Greed, Sneaky, Swarms, Gold, Weak, Hive, Shadows, Dark, Thieves, Shinies, Dungeon, Scout, Chaos, Low Level, Trash, Shinies, [[Goblincore]]

Goals:

Build a community game in the Dwarves vs. Goblins universe. Goal is for players to join and play core mini mechanics that will be used in the main game, with a core mechanic that shows the two communities competing. Make new kinds of gameplay that show innovation in game design.
